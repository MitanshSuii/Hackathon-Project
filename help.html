Help Center

Reporting Bullying

Go to the “Report Bullying” page to submit details about an incident. You can choose to share your name or stay anonymous. All reports are reviewed by school staff.

Private Support

>Use the “Private Chat” feature to talk directly with a counselor or teacher. These conversations are confidential and handled by trained professionals.

Witnessing Bullying

If you see bullying happening, please report it, even if you’re not involved. Speaking up helps protect other students and creates a safer school environment.

Technical Issues

If you are having trouble using any part of the website, try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection.

Need More Assistance?

Select “Contact a Teacher” for further help or questions. A staff member will respond as soon as possible.
